After college, I moved to Connecticut. I now live in Norwalk, CT. I have a daughter who is the center of my world and I would do anything for her.

More About Me

What sort of professional background do you have?

I graduated from Penn State University with a Bachelors of Science in Art Education. During my time at college, I focused on studio arts including graphic design. After graduating, I taught for a few years and even though I enjoyed the being with children I realized teaching was not for me. I started getting back into graphic design and began relearning the Adobe Creative Suite.

In 2010 I was hired by Penny-Dell Publications and learned Quark Xpress 6. I am a desktop publisher who designs both print and digital publications. Outside of work, I am learning Adobe CS 6, and front-end web design. I freelance for Digital Gravity Media, a SEO company based in Stamford, CT.

What is your dream job?

My dream job would be with a company where I could design, develop, maintain and update websites. I would want to be constantly challenged and given the opportuinity to learn new scripting languages and programs.

If you could do anything, what would you do?

I would travel the world and experience different cultures. I would love to try my hand at creating different types of cuisines. Looking at the art and architecture around the world would be fascinating.
